Section 6b
Interactive Calculators

The current site has no interactive tools. The new site ships two data-driven calculators that turn passive visitors into engaged prospects — and give the sales team a warm lead with context.

Risk Calculator
State-level disaster risk scores calibrated to FEMA, USGS & NOAA hazard data across all 50 states.
✓ Scores Fire · Earthquake · Flood · Wind risk per state (0–100)
✓ Personalizes the EHBG value prop to the prospect's actual location
✓ Triggered by "Calculate Your Risk" CTA — the most prominent nav button
✓ Creates urgency: prospects see their real exposure before seeing the solution
ROI Calculator
Houston neighborhood-level ROI model — inputs home value, sq ft, and zip area to project 25-year financial outcomes.
✓ 12 Houston micro-zones with real insurance multipliers (River Oaks, Meyerland, Galveston, etc.)
✓ Models insurance savings, energy savings, maintenance delta, and resale premium
✓ Outputs a dollar figure — turns "is this worth it?" into "I'd be losing money not to"
✓ "Consult" CTA fires directly from the result screen — highest-intent lead capture
Competitive Position
No other custom home builder in the Houston / Southern California market offers both a disaster risk assessment and a 25-year financial ROI model on their site. These tools don't just convert — they disqualify competitors before the prospect ever books a call.
