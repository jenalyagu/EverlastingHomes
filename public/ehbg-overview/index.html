Multi-select: Wildfire Protection · Seismic Resilience · Flood & Hurricane Defense · Energy Efficiency · Privacy & Security · Generational Legacy. Drives the personalized output.
4
Timeline
Within 6 months through Still Exploring. Segments the lead by urgency — critical for follow-up sequencing.

Contact Capture + Concept Generation
Name, email, state. On submit: a personalized estate concept brief is generated based on the prospect's priority selections — and sent to EHBG as a structured lead with full estate context attached.

Activation

The build is done.
Three steps to turn it on.

01
Set VITE_FORMSPREE_ID in Netlify
Create a Formspree account, copy the form ID into Netlify's environment variables, and redeploy. All consultation form submissions will route to the Formspree dashboard — no code changes needed.
→ Unlocks 4–6% lead capture rate on main site
02
Wire Estate Designer submission
Add a Formspree POST on the "Generate My Estate Concept" button in MyEverlastingHome.jsx. Send name, email, state, style, priorities, and timeline — every quiz completion becomes a structured lead.
→ Unlocks 25–40% quiz-to-consultation rate
03
Add intake link to Outreach Deck — Slide 8
A private advisor intake URL or dedicated phone number on the final slide. Advisors who want to make an introduction currently have no discreet path — this creates one.
→ Potential 3–4× improvement in advisor introduction rate
04
Schema markup on geo pages
Add LocalBusiness + Service schema to /luxury-homes-texas, /disaster-resistant-homes, and /wildfire-rebuild. Unlocks local map pack and rich result eligibility — no new content required.
→ Tier 4 location keyword rankings
05
Google Business Profile
Set up and optimize with completed SCIP build photos. Unlocks "near me" and local map pack results — the highest-intent traffic source available for a local custom builder.
→ "SCIP home builders near me" intent captured
06
Publish pillar content + NASIP listing
"What Is a SCIP Home?" blog post targets zero-competition informational keywords. NASIP Association listing provides the highest-value backlink in the SCIP space — available now.
→ Tier 3 education keywords + domain authority lift
