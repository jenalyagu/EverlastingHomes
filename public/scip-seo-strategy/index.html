Tier 1 — Why Low Volume Isn't Low Value

These searches are rare.
The buyers aren't.

Tier 1 keywords are low volume because SCIP is still a niche term — most $1M+ custom home buyers don't know the word yet. But the people who do search it are deep in the research phase, often with land already identified and a budget confirmed.

A single converted lead from "custom SCIP home builder" is worth more than 10,000 general traffic visits. These keywords should be owned outright — there is almost no competition and the buyer intent is explicit.
