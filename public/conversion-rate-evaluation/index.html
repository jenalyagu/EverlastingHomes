Asset 01 — Where Things Stand

Backend is coded.
Env var isn't set.

Formspree is fully implemented in the codebase — both on the main consultation modal and the contact form. The integration checks for VITE_FORMSPREE_ID at runtime.

No .env file exists and the Netlify environment variable is not set. Without it, every submission falls back to a mailto: link — the lead opens their email client but nothing is captured automatically.

  • Error handling and success state built
  • Mailto fallback prevents silent failures
  • Site is live and indexed

Asset 02 — Where Things Stand

Fully built.
No lead exits the page.

The 5-step quiz works beautifully — style, acreage, priorities, timeline, contact info. The concept summary on step 6 is personalized and high-quality. The main site links to it from two CTAs.

When a prospect hits "Generate My Estate Concept" on step 5, the app generates the summary locally and stops. There is no submission handler — the name and email collected in step 5 go nowhere. Every completed quiz is a lost lead.

Already Done
  • Full 5-step quiz built and deployed
  • Name + email collected at step 5
  • Personalized concept summary generated
  • Linked from main site (two CTAs)

Asset 03 — Analysis

Closest to working.
Dead end on slide 8.

An advisor receiving this deck in a warm context will likely read it — content is relevant, design is credible. The problem is what happens next.

Advisors who want to act have no mechanism to do so discreetly. Most won't do the extra work to find a contact. The few who convert are converting despite the deck, not because of it.

What's Working
  • Strong read rate in warm context
  • Content and design are credible
  • Warm referrals convert at 50–65% to consultation
Fix Required
  • Add private intake link to slide 8
  • Or: dedicated advisor phone line
  • Discreet, frictionless next step is the unlock
